Staff Positions

In our server you can find three Staff Positions - Departments

Staff Department

Staff Department or Staff Developer's job is answering tickets and making sure that everyone is following the rules and handle all violation issues. Staff Developers are supposed to be friendly, trustworthy but also hard-working and very active with a goal of making the server a better place for everyone.

Admin Department

They are responsible for the channels. They organize the announcements / polls / Question Of The Day and give ideas on what we should add / remove They also type the patch notes for every update. This allows them to be less active than a Staff Developer. They also have to answer the tickets.

Social Department

Their job is to get partnerships for our server, grow our social media, check each channel for clips and upload the "good ones" to our Official Social Media Accounts.
