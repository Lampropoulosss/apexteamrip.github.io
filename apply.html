RiP Central

We are glad that you are looking into applying as a Staff Member at RiP Central. Please read the following.

Every Staff Member starts as a Trial Staff Member and then they choose one of the following "Departments": Staff Developers | Admins | Social.

Staff Department:

Staff Department's or Staff Developer's job is answering tickets and making sure that everyone is following the rules and handle all violation issues. Staff Developers are supposed to be friendly, trustworthy but also hard-working and very active with a goal of making the server a better place for everyone.

Admin Department

They are responsible for the channels. They organize the Announcements / Polls / Question Of The Day / Patch Notes and give ideas on what we should add / remove. This allow you to be less active than a Staff Developer.

Social Department

Their job is to get partnerships for our server, grow our social media, check each channel for clips and upload some of them to our Official Social Media Accounts.

Note: As a Trial Staff Member you'll have 1 month to get promoted. Your job will be to be active and help the other staff members to do they job. If we see that you help we'll give you to choose between which "department" you want to join. If you don't get promoted means that you don't help the server as a staff member (You will be able to apply again after 1 month).

Requirements:

  • Minimum Age of 15
  • English Speaking
  • Be Active
